Privacy Policy

How we protect and use your personal information

Last updated: January 2024

Introduction

At Glamour Salon, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in compliance with GDPR and other privacy regulations.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and contact details
  • Appointment history and preferences
  • Payment information
  • Communication preferences

Service Information

  • Treatment preferences and history
  • Skin/hair type and concerns
  • Allergy information (for safety)
  • Before/after photos (with consent)

How We Use Your Information

  • Processing and managing bookings
  • Providing personalized services
  • Sending appointment reminders
  • Marketing communications (with consent)
  • Improving our services

Your Rights (GDPR)

Right to Access

Request a copy of your personal data

Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate personal data

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your data

Right to Portability

Export your data in a standard format

Data Security

  • SSL encryption for all data transfers
  • Secure payment processing (PCI compliant)
  • Regular security audits
  • Staff training on data protection

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion at any time.
