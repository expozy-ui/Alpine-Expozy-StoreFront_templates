Introduction

At LearnHub, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in compliance with GDPR and other applicable privacy regulations.

Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, and contact details
  • Profile picture and bio
  • Payment and billing information

Learning Data

  • Course enrollments and progress
  • Quiz scores and assignments
  • Certificates earned
  • Discussion forum participation

Usage Data

  • Video watch history

  • Feature usage patterns

How We Use Your Information

  • Providing and improving our learning platform
  • Personalizing course recommendations
  • Processing payments and preventing fraud
  • Sending course updates and notifications
  • Issuing certificates of completion

Your Rights (GDPR)

Data Security

We implement robust security measures including:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure payment processing (PCI compliant)
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
