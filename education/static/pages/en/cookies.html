Cookie Policy

How we use cookies on our platform

Last updated: January 2024

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our platform. They help us provide you with a better learning experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function properly:

  • User authentication and session management
  • Course progress tracking
  • Shopping cart functionality
  • Security and fraud prevention

Performance Cookies

These help us understand how students use our platform:

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Course completion rates
  • Video playback analytics
  • Error tracking and diagnostics

Functionality Cookies

These remember your preferences:

  • Language and region settings
  • Video quality preferences
  • Previously viewed courses
  • Learning progress bookmarks

Marketing Cookies

With your consent, these cookies help us:

  • Show relevant course recommendations
  • Measure advertising effectiveness
  • Deliver personalized content

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect functionality like course progress tracking and video playback.
