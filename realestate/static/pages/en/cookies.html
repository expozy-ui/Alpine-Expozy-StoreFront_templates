Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

How We Use Cookies

Our real estate website uses cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as:

  • Security and authentication
  • Session management
  • Saving your property search preferences
  • Remembering items in your saved properties list

Analytical Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Property listings viewed
  • Search queries and filter preferences
  • How you arrived at our website

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization:

  • Remembering your language preferences
  • Storing your recently viewed properties
  • Customizing your property recommendations
  • Remembering your contact information for inquiries

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements:

  • Showing you property listings that match your interests
  • Retargeting campaigns on other platforms
  • Measuring the effectiveness of our advertising

Third-Party Cookies

We may use services from third parties that set their own cookies, including:

  • Google Analytics - For website analytics
  • Google Maps - For displaying property locations
  • Social Media Platforms - For sharing functionality
  • Payment Processors - For secure transactions

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete all or specific cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Our Cookie Consent Tool

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on our website:

  • You may need to log in each time you visit
  • Your saved properties list may not persist
  • Search preferences may not be remembered
  • Some features may not work as intended
