Our Beginning

From Humble Origins to Culinary Excellence

What began as a small family kitchen in 1995 has blossomed into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations. Our founders, Maria and Giovanni, brought their grandmother's recipes from the rolling hills of Tuscany, infusing each dish with generations of culinary tradition.

Today, while we've grown and evolved, our commitment to those original values remains unchanged: fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, time-honored cooking techniques, and an unwavering dedication to creating memorable dining experiences.

Every dish that leaves our kitchen tells a story – of the farmers who grew the produce, the artisans who crafted the ingredients, and the passion of our chefs who bring it all together.

Executive Chef

Chef Alessandro Rossi

With over 20 years of culinary experience across three continents, Chef Alessandro brings a unique perspective to our kitchen. Trained at Le Cordon Bleu in Paris and having worked in Michelin-starred restaurants in Italy and Japan, he combines classical techniques with innovative approaches.

"Cooking is about telling stories. Every ingredient has a history, and every dish should transport you somewhere meaningful. My goal is to create food that not only satisfies the palate but also touches the soul."
