Membership terms and gym rules

Last updated: January 2024

1. Membership Agreement

1.1 Eligibility

To become a member, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years old (or 16 with parental consent)
  • Complete the membership application form truthfully
  • Provide valid identification and payment information
  • Agree to these terms and conditions

1.2 Membership Types

We offer various membership options including:

  • Basic - Access to gym equipment during standard hours
  • Premium - Full access including group classes
  • VIP - Unlimited access plus personal training sessions

1.3 Membership Duration

Memberships are available as:

  • Monthly (auto-renewing)
  • Annual (12-month commitment)
  • Pay-as-you-go day passes

2. Payment Terms

2.1 Fees

  • Membership fees are due on the same date each month
  • Annual memberships must be paid in full at signup or in monthly installments
  • All prices are inclusive of applicable taxes

2.2 Payment Methods

We accept credit/debit cards and direct bank transfers. Cash payments are accepted for day passes only.

2.3 Failed Payments

If a payment fails:

  • We will attempt to process the payment again within 3 days
  • A late fee of $15 may be applied after 7 days
  • Access may be suspended after 14 days of non-payment

3. Cancellation Policy

3.1 Monthly Memberships

  • Cancel anytime with 30 days written notice
  • No cancellation fees apply
  • Final payment will be processed on your next billing date

3.2 Annual Memberships

  • Early cancellation within first 14 days: full refund
  • Early cancellation after 14 days: 50% of remaining balance
  • Medical cancellation: full refund with doctor's certificate

3.3 Membership Freeze

You may freeze your membership:

  • Up to 3 months per year at no charge
  • Minimum freeze period: 1 month
  • Request must be submitted 7 days in advance

4. Gym Rules & Conduct

4.1 General Rules

  • Always carry and scan your membership card or app
  • Wear appropriate athletic attire and closed-toe shoes
  • Bring a towel and wipe down equipment after use
  • Re-rack weights and return equipment to proper locations
  • Limit equipment use to 30 minutes during peak hours
  • No photography or recording without permission

4.2 Prohibited Behavior

  • Harassment or intimidation of members or staff
  • Use of illegal substances or alcohol on premises
  • Dropping or throwing weights
  • Personal training without authorization
  • Selling goods or services on premises

4.3 Violations

Violation of gym rules may result in:

  • Verbal warning
  • Written warning
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent membership termination (no refund)

5. Class Bookings

  • Classes must be booked in advance through our app or website
  • Cancellations must be made at least 2 hours before class
  • No-shows may be charged a $10 fee after 3 occurrences
  • Late arrivals may not be admitted for safety reasons

6. Personal Training

  • Personal training sessions expire 6 months from purchase
  • Cancellations require 24 hours notice
  • Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours notice are forfeited
  • Personal training packages are non-refundable
