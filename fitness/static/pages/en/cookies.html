Cookie Policy

How we use cookies on our website

Last updated: January 2024

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as:

  • User authentication and session management
  • Class booking functionality
  • Shopping cart for merchandise purchases
  • Security features

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Error messages encountered
  • Load times and site performance

Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow us to remember your choices and provide enhanced features:

  • Language preferences
  • Membership preferences
  • Workout preferences and goals
  • Class booking history

Marketing Cookies

These cookies may be set by our advertising partners to:

  • Build a profile of your interests
  • Show you relevant ads on other websites
  • Limit the number of times you see an ad
  • Measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics - for website analytics
  • Facebook Pixel - for social media integration
  • Stripe - for secure payment processing
  • YouTube - for embedded workout videos

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Delete all or specific cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies only
  • Accept cookies from specific sites

Cookie Consent Tool

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner that allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies.

Impact of Blocking Cookies

If you choose to block certain cookies, please note that some features of our website may not work properly:

  • You may not be able to stay logged in
  • Class booking may not function correctly
  • Your preferences won't be saved
  • Some workout videos may not load

Cookie Retention

Different cookies are stored for different periods:

  • Session cookies - deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies - stored for a set period (typically 30 days to 2 years)
  • Authentication cookies - stored for 30 days with "remember me" enabled
