Hotel Expozy

Hotel Expozy is located in the center of Sofia and offers style, comfort and an excellent location. With modern rooms and professional service, it is ideal for both business trips and leisure. A cozy atmosphere and attention to every detail await you.

The rooms have a balcony and offer a choice of a double bed or two single beds. Amenities include free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen smart TV, air conditioning, a safe, an electric kettle, a minibar, Ness coffee and tea. Each room has a luxurious bathroom with a shower, premium hotel cosmetics and a hairdryer.

Guests can enjoy complimentary welcome water, and families with children can request a free baby cot with bedding. Smoking is only allowed on the balconies, while all indoor areas are non-smoking.
