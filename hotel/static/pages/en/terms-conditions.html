Terms and Conditions

Visitor Policy

Guests are allowed to invite visitors to their room from 06:00 - 22:00. If the visitor stays in the room after 22:00, the visitor must register by presenting a passport or ID card. The hotel is not responsible for any crimes related to unregistered visitors.

Pet Policy

Our pet policy does not allow pets in public areas, restaurants, bars, Spa and Wellness Center and conference rooms. Passing through public areas with pets is only permitted when entering and exiting your hotel room. If you wish to bring a pet, you must inform the hotel at least 24 hours prior to your arrival. There is an additional fee of EUR 50 per day per pet. Pets must be accompanied by a guest and kept on a leash or in a carrier bag/box at all times. Please note that we only accept small to medium-sized pets (up to 10 kg).

Smoking Policy

The entire hotel building, including rooms and apartments, is non-smoking. We appreciate your cooperation with our smoking policy and reserve the right to charge a cleaning fee of EUR 200 to any guest who violates this policy. We offer several terraces at some of our dining venues where you can smoke. The main smoking area is in front of the hotel's main entrance.

Candles, Incense Sticks and Essential Oils Policy

Candles, incense sticks, essential oils (diffusers, vaporizers, etc.) are prohibited. The use of these items will be treated as smoking, will result in a fine of EUR 200, and the guest may be evicted without a refund.

Cooking, Cooking Appliances, Fuels or Fireworks Policy

The safety of our guests, staff and the hotel is of utmost importance to us. Cooking in guest rooms using any type of cooking appliance is prohibited. A minimum fee of EUR 200 will be charged for cooking in a room, including but not limited to the use of stoves, toaster ovens, water heaters, rice cookers, flammable substances, open flames, barbecue grills, burners, heaters or other items intended for cooking. Open fires, flames or cooking grills, whether charcoal or gas, and fireworks are not permitted anywhere in the hotel. Please always order food and coffee from room service or the restaurants. If damage occurs to hotel property, the guest is responsible for covering the full cost of the items.

Day Use Policy

Guests can book a hotel room for the day. These rooms can only be occupied between 10:00 AM and 7:00 PM. The price of a day use room includes the room rental for the specific time period. The guest can check in at any time during this period and must vacate the room no later than 19:00.

Video surveillance policy

Video surveillance cameras (CCTV) monitor the hotel's territory. All information is recorded, verified on digital media and stored. Video surveillance is carried out to ensure the safety of guests, their property, as well as the property of the hotel. By signing the registration card, upon check-in, guests acknowledge and agree to the  use of a video surveillance system on the hotel premises (excluding rooms).

Firearms policy

The hotel prohibits any guest or client from carrying firearms, ammunition, knives or weapons of any kind on the hotel premises. This policy applies to all firearms, whether pistols or long-barreled weapons. Guests who do not comply with these rules will be asked to leave the hotel.

Luggage Storage Policy

Depending on occupancy, guests may store their luggage, at their own risk for loss or damage for any reason, in the luggage room. Luggage cannot be stored for more than 30 days. The hotel reserves the right to refuse storage. Luggage storage is only permitted for items that belong to guests arriving at the hotel with a confirmed reservation or who have had a reservation and have left their room. In theThe luggage storage facility is not permitted to store guests' luggage or belongings from other commercial establishments.

In-room safe policy

The hotel is not responsible for damage or loss of money, valuables, securities, jewelry and other items of the guest that are not stored and locked in the safe provided in the guest's bedroom.

Lost property policy

In case of discovery of forgotten belongings of the guest, the hotel immediately notifies the owner, if known. Forgotten items are stored at the hotel for 6 months from the date of their discovery. After this period has expired and the owner of the forgotten items has not been found, the hotel has the right to use, discard or destroy the items.

Property Damage Policy

The guest will be responsible for any loss or damage to hotel property caused by themselves, their guests or any person for whom they are responsible. Damages found after departure - rooms found with litter, in complete disarray and/or "destroyed" will be subject to a deep cleaning fee of 200 euros and/or third party fees, if necessary.

Management Rights Policy

It is agreed that the guest will behave in a respectful manner and will not cause any nuisance on the hotel grounds. The management has the right to ask any guest to vacate their room or other parts of the hotel immediately, without prior notice and without giving any reason, and the guest is obliged to leave when requested to do so. If the situation escalates and the guest does not comply with this policy, the hotel will call the police.

The hotel has a zero tolerance policy, whereby we will refuse service or accommodation at our hotel or may remove any person who refuses to comply with reasonable standards and policies established by the Republic of Bulgaria and the hotel management. During epidemics, we reserve the right to implement precautions at our discretion or as required by local authorities. We may charge you a room cleaning fee as we deem appropriate under the circumstances.
