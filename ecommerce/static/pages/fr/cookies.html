Que sont les cookies?

“Бисквитката” are small text files, consisting of letters and numbers, that will be stored on your computer, mobile phone or other terminal equipment of the user, through which they access the internet. The cookie is installed through a request, sent from the user terminal to the server or to a third party server.

À quoi servent les cookies?

These files enable, generally, recognition of the user terminal and presentation of content in a way, that is relevant, adapted to user preferences. Cookies ensure, that users have a good experience and support efforts to provide services, best suited to users, for example regarding preferences for online privacy, shopping cart or relevant advertisements. Also, they are used in preparing anonymous aggregated statistics, that help us understand how users benefit from our web pages, allowing us to improve the structure and content, without identifying each individual user.

Quels types de cookies utilisons-nous?

We use two types of cookies: de session et persistants. Session cookies are temporary files, that remain on the terminal until the user session ends or the application is closed. Persistent cookies remain on the user terminal for the period, determined by the cookie parameters or until manually deleted by the user.

Do cookies or similar technologies contain personal data?

Cookies or other similar technologies themselves do not require personal information, to be used and in many cases, do not even identify internet users. However, there are situations, when personal data may be collected through the use of cookies to facilitate some user features or to provide the user with an experience, that is tailored to their preferences. This data is encrypted in a way, that makes unauthorized access impossible.

Why are cookies and/or other similar technologies important for the Internet?

Cookies and/or other similar technologies occupy a central place for the efficient functioning of the internet, helping to create a friendly directed experience and stay, that is adapted to the interests and preferences of each user. Refusing or Disabling cookies may make some sites or parts of sites unable to be used.
Disabling cookies does not mean, that you will no longer receive, according to legislation, online advertising, but only, that it will no longer take into account your preferences and interests, visible from your browsing behavior on the Internet.
Examples of important need to use cookies (which do not require user permission through the account):
Content and services, tailored to user preferences – product and service categories.
Offers, tailored to user interests
Remembering passwords
Remembering child protection filters for Internet content (family mode options (family mode), safe search function (safe search)).
Limiting the frequency of ad distribution – limiting the number of times an ad is shown to a particular user on a site.
Providing relevant advertising to the user.
Measurement, optimization and adaptation of analysis characteristics – such as confirmation of a certain level of traffic on the website, what type of content is viewed and how a user enters the website (for example: through search engines, directly, from other websites, etc.). Websites process these analytics about their use to improve services for users.
