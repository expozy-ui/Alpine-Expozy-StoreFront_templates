Introduction

Your privacy and the security of your health information are paramount to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal and medical information in compliance with HIPAA, GDPR, and other applicable regulations.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name, date of birth, contact information
  • Insurance and billing information

Protected Health Information (PHI)

  • Medical history and diagnoses
  • Treatment records and prescriptions
  • Lab results and imaging reports
  • Appointment and visit records

How We Use Your Information

  • Providing medical treatment and care
  • Processing insurance claims and billing
  • Appointment scheduling and reminders
  • Quality improvement and safety initiatives
  • Legal compliance and reporting

Data Security

  • Regular security audits and staff training
